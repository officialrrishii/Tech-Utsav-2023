About Event & Rules

Technical

Technical events can cover a wide range of topics and fields, such as Computer science/Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, and more. These events involve coding challenges, hackathons, robotics competitions, technical quizzes and more that help participants learn new skills and technologies.
Technical Events can also help participants develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork skills that are essential for success in technical fields.

Non-Technical

Non-technical event is an event that focused on social, cultural, or recreational activities. These events are designed to provide entertainment, promote socialization, and build a sense of community among participants.
Non-technical events play an important role in building a sense of community and promoting socialization among participants. They provide an opportunity for people to connect, share their interests, and learn from one another.
